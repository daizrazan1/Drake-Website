About Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He grew up in the Forest Hill neighborhood, raised by his mother, Sandi Graham, after his parents’ divorce. Drake attended Forest Hill Collegiate Institute and later Vaughan Road Academy, where he began acting, notably as Jimmy Brooks on *Degrassi: The Next Generation*.

Transitioning to music, Drake released his debut mixtape, *Room for Improvement*, in 2006, followed by *Comeback Season* in 2007. His breakthrough came with *So Far Gone* in 2009, leading to a deal with Young Money Entertainment. His discography includes iconic albums like *Take Care*, *Nothing Was the Same*, and *Views*, blending hip-hop, R&B, and pop.

Drake has won 5 Grammy Awards, 34 Billboard Music Awards, 6 American Music Awards, and holds records like the most Billboard Hot 100 entries (331 as of 2023). With over 170 million records sold, he’s one of the best-selling artists ever.
